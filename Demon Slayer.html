Demon Slayer : Kimetsu No Yabi is a widely acclaimed anime and manga series following Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy who becomes a demon slayer after a demon slaughters his family and turns his sister, Nezuko, into a demon. Ufotable studios produces the series, known for breathtaking, high-quality animation during fight scenes. Alongside friends Zenitsu and Inosuke, Tanjiro fights against Muzan Kibutsuji's elite demons to cure Nezuko and protect humanity.
